Medics in Web3 Event

The MEDICSINWEB3DAO hosted its inaugural in-real-life (IRL) event in June 2024, marking a significant milestone for the organization and its community. This event aimed to bring together enthusiasts, professionals, and advocates in the Web3 and healthcare sectors to explore the intersection of technology and medicine. With over 400 participants, 6 speakers, 3 sponsors and 5 community partners the event fostered networking, knowledge sharing, and collaboration among attendees.

Medics in Web3 Walk

Medics in Web3 Walk The Medics in Web3 Walk brought together healthcare professionals, students, blockchain enthusiasts, and community members to raise awareness on how Web3 can transform healthcare. Participants marched with banners and shared messages on transparency, innovation, and decentralization in health systems. The event also created space for networking, knowledge sharing, and partnerships across health and tech sectors.The walk boosted visibility for Medics in Web3 DAO, increased community awareness, and strengthened the movement towards blockchain-powered healthcare solutions.
