Decentralizing Community Led Healthcare Powered By Web3 For All.

Medics in Web3 DAO is a community of healthcare professionals exploring how blockchain technology and its use cases can address pressing challenges in the health sector. We believe that decentralization, transparency, and innovation can reshape healthcare systems to become more efficient, accessible, and secure.

Medics in Web3 Event

In June 2024, MEDICSINWEB3DAO held its first IRL event, bringing together over 400 participants, 6 speakers, 3 sponsors, and 5 community partners to explore the intersection of Web3 and healthcare, fostering networking, collaboration, and knowledge sharing.

Medics in Web3 Event

MEDICSINWEB3DAO conducted a major health outreach in BamBam, Balanga LGA of Gombe State, screening over 500 people for Hepatitis B and C while also introducing about 60% of the community to Web3 technology.

Medics in Web3 Walk

The Medics in Web3 Walk gathered health professionals, students, and blockchain enthusiasts to promote Web3's role in healthcare. Participants advocated for transparency, innovation, and decentralization in health systems.

What is a DAO?

A DAO, or Decentralized Autonomous Organization, is a blockchain-based system that enables collective decision-making and management through smart contracts and distributed governance.

Transparency

Patient data and medical records secured through immutable blockchain technology.

Democratic Governance

Decentralized decision-making where healthcare professionals and communities vote on initiatives.

Immutable

Connecting healthcare providers worldwide to deliver care where it's needed most.

Token based incentives

Smart contracts enable immediate funding release for verified medical interventions.
